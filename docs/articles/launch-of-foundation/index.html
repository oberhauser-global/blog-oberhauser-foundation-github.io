Oberhauser Foundation Launch

By Alex Oberhauser

June 29, 2020 - 1 minutes read - 109 words

Boston, Massachusetts USA — June 29th, 2020 — The Oberhauser Foundation was established under the charter of the Oberhauser Dynasty in the midst of the global COVID-19 pandemic and a global economical crisis.

The main directive of the foundation is to manage the endowment for charitable giving and to align the yearly issued grants with the values of the donors and the mission of the Oberhauser Dynasty.

The created structures facilitate sustainable giving that can be carried on by future generations. By pooling resources, grants can be directed in a more efficient and targeted way.

Learn more under https://oberhauser.foundation and stay tuned for more news articles published on https://blog.oberhauser.foundation.
